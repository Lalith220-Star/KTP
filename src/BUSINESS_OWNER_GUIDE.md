## Understanding Your LBH Score Components

Your score is calculated from 6 weighted factors:

| Factor | Weight | What It Measures |
|--------|--------|------------------|
| **Food Quality** | 30% | Taste, freshness, presentation, consistency |
| **Service** | 25% | Staff friendliness, speed, attentiveness |
| **Ambiance** | 15% | Atmosphere, decor, comfort, noise level |
| **Value for Money** | 15% | Pricing vs. quality and portion sizes |
| **Cleanliness** | 10% | Hygiene, maintenance, presentation |
| **Location** | 5% | Accessibility, parking, convenience |

**Why These Weights?**
- Food Quality and Service have the highest impact on customer satisfaction and repeat business
- Ambiance and Value significantly influence decision-making
- Cleanliness and Location are important but typically less variable

## Common Scenarios & Solutions

### Scenario 1: "My score just dropped 5 points!"
**What to Do:**
1. Go to "Operational Insights" tab immediately
2. Look for new critical/high priority issues
3. Check "Score Trends" to see which component dropped
4. Review recent public data signals (new reviews, job posts)
5. Implement highest priority recommendation

### Scenario 2: "I'm below average in Service"
**What to Do:**
1. Go to "Competitive Benchmark" tab
2. See exactly how far below average you are
3. Go to "Operational Insights" for root cause
4. Check if high staff turnover is indicated (job postings)
5. Implement staff training/retention recommendations

### Scenario 3: "I made changes but score hasn't improved"
**What to Do:**
1. Check "Score Trends" - improvements take 2-4 weeks
2. Ensure you addressed the right issue (use Operational Insights)
3. Verify implementation was complete
4. Some fixes (like ambiance upgrades) take 60-90 days

### Scenario 4: "I want to beat my competitor"
**What to Do:**
1. Add competitor to "Watchlist"
2. Compare component scores in "Competitive Benchmark"
3. Identify where they're stronger than you
4. Focus improvements on those specific areas
5. Track relative progress monthly

## Reading Data Signals

### Review Volume Trending Down?
**Possible Causes:**
- Reduced customer traffic
- Not asking for reviews
- Seasonal slowdown

**What to Check:**
- Operating hours (any recent changes?)
- Local competition (new restaurants nearby?)
- Recent menu/pricing changes

### Multiple Job Postings Detected?
**What This Means:**
- Staff turnover likely increased
- Service quality may suffer
- Training costs rising

**What to Do:**
- Review retention strategies
- Check if Service score is declining
- Implement better onboarding

### Operating Hours Changes?
**What This Signals:**
- Staffing issues
- Demand changes
- Operational challenges

**What to Check:**
- Whether changes align with customer demand
- Impact on customer convenience
- Staff scheduling problems

## Success Metrics to Track

Over 3-6 months, track:

1. **LBH Score Improvement**
   - Goal: +10-15 points in 6 months
   - Focus on lowest-scoring components first

2. **Competitive Position**
   - Goal: Move from bottom 50% to top 25%
   - Benchmark monthly

3. **Critical Issues**
   - Goal: Reduce from 3-4 to 0-1
   - Address highest priority first

4. **Specific Component Gains**
   - Goal: Bring all components above category average
   - Focus on weighted factors (Food, Service)
